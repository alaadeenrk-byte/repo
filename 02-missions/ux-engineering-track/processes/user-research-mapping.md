## Description
User Research & Mapping — first step in the UX Engineering track. Runs 5-8 discovery interviews with school owners/staff via Souhibe's network, then converts that raw evidence into empathy maps (Think/Feel/Say/Do), a current-state journey map of how schools manage things today (pre-CourseLi), an impact × feasibility opportunity matrix, and persona cards with behavioral triggers. This is the UX-side counterpart to the Strategist's JTBD research — same users, different questions — and feeds directly into the Convergence cross-check.
