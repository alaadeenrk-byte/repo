## Description
Online Presence Build — fourth and final step in the UX Engineering track. Builds the landing page hierarchy (hero → problem → solution → social proof → CTA), pressure-tests it with an above-fold 5-second comprehension check, and lays the SEO/analytics groundwork (meta/OG/schema, GA4 + heatmaps + session recording) before anything goes live — plus a waitlist hook with a real value exchange, not a bare signup form. Closes with the actual launch: website deployed live, social accounts secured, business email set up, and every artifact compiled into deliverables (component library, SEO report, online presence audit).
