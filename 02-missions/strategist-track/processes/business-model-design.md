## Description
Business Model Design — third step in the Strategist track. Turns the Discovery evidence into an actual model: confirms subscription as the revenue model with written rationale, builds the unit economics model (CAC, LTV, payback, gross margin, scenarios), locks the 3-tier pricing architecture off the 100-150 DZD/student/month figure, decides the GTM motion (PLG/SLG/blended), and states a falsifiable 90-day revenue hypothesis with kill criteria. Compiles into the full business plan, canvas, pricing doc, GTM doc, budget breakdown, and one-pager — pitch deck only if fundraising is actually on the table.
