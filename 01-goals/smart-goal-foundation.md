## Goal
Complete the Agency OS Foundation phase in full — both tracks, all 8 processes,
every internal database item and every external deliverable defined in the
framework — producing a documented, evidence-backed go/no-go decision on
whether CourseLi is worth building.

Scope is complete, not compressed. Timing is directional (~1 week) and may
extend; do not cut scope to fit the clock.

## Completion Condition
This goal is considered **complete when, and only when, both child Missions
— `strategist-track` and `ux-engineering-track` — have closed as `done`**
(each mission itself closes only once all its Processes close, per the
standard state machine).

Reaching that point does not auto-close this goal. As with every level in
this system, closing the goal itself requires an explicit verification
meeting between Alaaedeen and Dazdaz, with `meeting_date` and `verified_by`
filled in above — Claude may flag that both missions show `done` and
prompt for this meeting, but cannot record the goal as closed itself.

## What the Meeting Actually Confirms
Not just "did both missions finish" (that's mechanical), but the substantive
question this whole phase exists to answer: **does the evidence gathered
support a go decision on building CourseLi, or not.** The Success Criteria
below are the inputs to that judgment, not a replacement for it — it's
possible for every checkbox to be technically satisfied and for the honest
answer at the meeting to still be "the evidence is weak, we need more time
before deciding."

## Success Criteria (evidence the completion-condition meeting draws on)
- All 8 processes across both tracks have their tasks closed as `done` or
  `done-with-gaps` (with gaps resolved)
- All Strategist external deliverables compiled (Feasibility Study, Full
  Business Plan, Business Model Canvas, Pricing Strategy Document, GTM
  Strategy Document, Budget Breakdown, One-Pager, Validation Plan, Investor
  Pitch Deck if applicable)
- All UX Engineering external deliverables compiled (Brand Identity Package
  registered, Website Live, Social Media Platforms, Business Email System,
  Component Library, SEO Foundation Report, Online Presence Audit)
- A single convergence document produced, synthesizing both tracks into one
  go/no-go recommendation for CourseLi
