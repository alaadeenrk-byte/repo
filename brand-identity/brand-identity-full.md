## Company Foundation
CourseLi opened in Alger Centre in 2021 on the observation that Algeria's
universities and job market speak two different languages — graduates leave
with diplomas, employers ask for skills. In-person, small-cohort, intensive
programs, built to be used at work the following Monday. No recorded
lectures, no self-paced modules. Single physical institute (Rue des Frères
Bouadou, Bir Mourad Raïs), instructors with 8+ years active field experience.
Current state: operating since 2021, 1,200+ students trained, 5 categories
(Web Development, Design, Business, Languages, Marketing).

## Vision & Business Model
Vision: close the distance between what Algeria teaches and what Algeria's
employers need. Delivery: 100% in-person, expert-led, capped at 25
students/cohort, evenings and weekends. Support: WhatsApp-first, under an
hour response time. Promise: "Real instructors. Real classrooms. Real
careers."

## Naming — Decision Process
10 candidates evaluated: Massar (مسار), Takwin (تكوين), Mahara (مهارة),
Atelia, Cursa, Najahi (نجاحي), Formatis, Skilla, Progressa, and **CourseLi
(selected)**.

**Why CourseLi:** direct, bilingual, reads naturally in French, English, and
transliterated Arabic. Deconstruction: "Course" — the program itself,
understood instantly in French/English. "Li" — in Arabic, لي (li) means
"mine/for me." The name is a promise in two languages at once: this course,
made for you.

## Brand Slogan
- Primary (English): "Unlock Your Potential"
- Secondary (French): "Formation en présentiel, résultats bien réels"

## Logo — Decision Process
Three directions explored:
1. **C as Open Book** — rejected, too expected for an education brand
2. **C as Mortarboard** — rejected, points backward to the ceremony, not the work
3. **C as Progress Ring + Play (SELECTED)** — an open ring standing in for
   the letter C, with a play triangle set into the gap. Reads three ways in
   under a second: as a C, as a play/start button, and as a progress ring
   nearly complete.

Geometric logic: the ring's opening and the triangle's point share the same
angle — drawn together, not assembled separately. All terminals rounded,
matching the product's UI corner radius.

Versions: Primary (blue wordmark, light backgrounds), Reversed (white
wordmark, dark backgrounds/app headers), Mark Only (ring-and-play icon —
favicon, app icon, WhatsApp avatar).

## Typography
Single family: **Inter**, all weights (Light 300 for body, Medium 500 for UI
labels, Bold 700 for emphasis/wordmark, Black 900 for headlines/stat
numbers). Rationale: built for screens, removes the seam between marketing
site and student portal. Tabular numerals used for all live stats (student
counts, satisfaction rate) so digits align and don't jitter.

## Color System
| Role | Hex | Usage |
|---|---|---|
| Primary Blue | #1558F6 | Trust, focus, action |
| Blue Dark | #0D3FCC | Depth, hover states |
| Ink | #0A0F1E | Contrast, text, dark UI |
| Blue Light | #EEF3FF | Backgrounds, soft surfaces |

Usage ratios: White 55% / Blue 30% / Ink 10% / Blue Light 5%.
